About us

Our journey began with a simple love for baking. What started as a hobby in our home kitchen soon blossomed into a full-fledged bakery, fueled by our passion for creating delicious, handcrafted delights.

With every recipe perfected and every ingredient carefully selected, we've poured our hearts into building a bakery that stands for quality, creativity, and community. Today, Coffeehouse Bakery has over 50 locations in the UK.
